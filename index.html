Methodology. Indicative early-design estimates of A1–A3 (product stage / cradle-to-gate) embodied carbon only. For volume-based materials, carbon = thickness × density × factor, per m² of detail. Biogenic carbon, transport (A4), construction, in-use and end-of-life stages are not included.

Data. Each factor's source is shown under its layer row. Values are representative cradle-to-gate averages in the style of the ICE database (Inventory of Carbon & Energy, Circular Ecology) and are indicative only. Every factor is editable — override it with a value from a product-specific EPD (Environmental Product Declaration) before relying on results. Not a substitute for a full LCA.
