Methodology. Indicative early-design estimates of A1–A3 (product stage / cradle-to-gate) embodied carbon only. For volume-based materials, carbon = thickness × density × factor, per m² of detail. Biogenic carbon, transport (A4), construction, in-use and end-of-life stages are not included.

Data. Factors are representative averages (indicative, ICE v3–style) and are editable — override them with product-specific EPDs before relying on results. Not a substitute for a full LCA.
